===== FVRG Departure =====

  * FVRG doesn't have a dedicated Departure Position, with Harare Approach (FVRG APP) assuming the role of Departure.


==== Departure Procedures ====

  * After departure, maintain aircraft on runway heading. You may issue further climb instructions (usually FL150).
  * If necessary, vector aircraft right (runway 05 departures) or left (runway 23 departures)
  * Be aware of High Terrain (MSA of 10900 ft) SE of the field.
