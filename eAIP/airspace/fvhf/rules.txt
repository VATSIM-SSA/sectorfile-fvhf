===== FVHF Harare Airspace Rules =====

==== Separation ====

  * Lateral Separation of 100 NM
  * Longitudinal Separation of 20 Mins or 10 Mins if routes are RNP10 approved.

=== Vertical Separation ===

  * FVHF Harare/Zimbabwe does not apply RVSM airspace separation so ICAO standards are applicable.



==== SELCAL ====

  * FVHF Harare does NOT offer SELCAL capabilities.

==== SLOP ====

  * The use of SLOP at FVHF Harare is NOT approved.
